Simulate your deck against another deck

>tu_optimize.exe "Alaric, Revolver(3)" "Halcyon, Barrage Tank(3)" sim 100000
Your Deck: [REH3+j] Alaric, Revolver, Revolver, Revolver
Enemy's Deck: [QEFj+j] Halcyon, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank
win%: 99.572 (99572 / 100000)
stall%: 0 (0 / 100000)
loss%: 0.428 (428 / 100000)
  • cards are identified by their name. By definition Halcyon means Halcyon Level 6. If you want to use Halycon Level 5 use Halcyon-5
  • if you have eg. three copies of Revolver use Revolver(3)
  • by default your deck and enemy's deck are played in random order

Play your deck ordered

>tu_optimize.exe "Alaric, Revolver(3)" "Halcyon, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank" -r sim 100000
Your Deck: [REH3+j] Alaric, Revolver, Revolver, Revolver
Enemy's Deck: [QEFj+j] Halcyon, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank
win%: 99.572 (99572 / 100000)
stall%: 0 (0 / 100000)
loss%: 0.428 (428 / 100000)
  • -r tries to play your card in the correct order still following the "three card hand limit" rule
  • by default normal PVP mode is used. That means your deck starts in turn 1

Play your deck in surge mode

>tu_optimize.exe "Alaric, Revolver(3)" "Halcyon, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank" -r sim 100000
Your Deck: [REH3+j] Alaric, Revolver, Revolver, Revolver
Enemy's Deck: [QEFj+j] Halcyon, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank, Barrage Tank
win%: 99.572 (99572 / 100000)
stall%: 0 (0 / 100000)
loss%: 0.428 (428 / 100000)
  • -s means enemy's deck starts in turn 1
